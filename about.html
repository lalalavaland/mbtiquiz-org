About MBTIQuiz.org

A free personality assessment tool helping people understand themselves and others through the MBTI framework.

Our Mission

At mbtiquiz.org, we believe that self-awareness is one of the most powerful tools for personal growth. Our mission is to make the Myers-Briggs Type Indicator framework accessible to everyone, completely free of charge, so that more people can discover insights about their personality, strengths, and how they relate to the world around them.

We created mbtiquiz.org because we saw a need for a straightforward, well-designed, and ad-supported personality test that delivers genuine value without hidden paywalls or upsells. Whether you are discovering MBTI for the first time or revisiting your type after years of personal growth, our goal is to give you an experience that is both informative and enjoyable.

What We Offer

Free MBTI Personality Quiz

Our core offering is a free, carefully crafted personality quiz based on the MBTI framework. The quiz is designed to help you identify your preferences across the four MBTI dichotomies: Extraversion vs. Introversion, Sensing vs. Intuition, Thinking vs. Feeling, and Judging vs. Perceiving. Your combination of preferences produces one of 16 personality types, each with distinct strengths, communication styles, and growth areas.

In-Depth Personality Type Guides

Beyond the quiz itself, we provide detailed guides for each of the 16 personality types. These guides cover core traits, ideal career paths, relationship compatibility, famous figures who share your type, and actionable tips for personal development.

Educational Blog Content

Our blog features articles on MBTI compatibility, career guidance by type, the science behind personality frameworks, and practical advice for applying personality insights in everyday life. We publish content in both English and Korean to serve our diverse, international audience.

Our Values

Free for Everyone

Personality insights should be accessible to all. Our quiz and content will always be free.

Privacy First

Your quiz answers stay in your browser. We do not store or sell personal data.

Educational Integrity

We present MBTI as a tool for self-reflection, not as settled science or clinical diagnosis.

Continuous Improvement

We regularly update our quiz, content, and research to reflect the latest understanding of personality.
